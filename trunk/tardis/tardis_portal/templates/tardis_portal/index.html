xTARDIS is based on MyTARDIS, a multi-institutional collaborative venture that facilitates the archiving and sharing of data and metadata collected at major facilities such as the Australian Synchrotron and ANSTO and within several institutions.

xTARDIS is developed by the University of South Australia through funding provided by ANDS. It endevours to extend the core of MyTARDIS, and enable a simple, easy to use data and meta-data repository for a wider range of research institutions. Priorities are:

  • RIF-CS harvesting via OAI-PMH for the Research Data Australia database.
  • Automatic meta-data extraction from experiments.
  • Centralised storage of all raw experiment data to facilitate automated backup and recovery.
  • Ease of use for researchers and administrators.

For more information on the xTARDIS project please contact Ryan Green.
For more information on the MyTARDIS project please contact Associate Professor Ashley Buckle.
